WORKSHOPS & MENTORING

In January 2018 I was asked to teach wedding photography for a photography class. That experience was something that added flavor, and thus came the idea of ​doing workshops as well. I started Northerners Workshops, and in the fall of 2018 I had my first workshop for 15 photographers together with Christin Eide at Håholmen. The last workshop was in Ålesund in the fall of 2022. I plan to do more, so stay tuned here for info on future workshops!
Now I also offer 1-1 mentoring, for those who want a more personal follow-up on the way to developing as a wedding photographer. Get in touch to get more information about mentoring!
