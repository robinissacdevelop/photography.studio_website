MOMENTS YOU WILL REMEMBER

If you are inside and looking at the website here, maybe it means that you are newly engaged...? And then I reckon that this isn't the first website with wedding photos you've looked at either, and that's good! It's a good idea to have a look around and be sure of the kind of style and expression you want in your wedding photos. I have a documentary style and a mix of portraits and natural moments bring out all sides of your big day. Feel free to take a look under Blog - Weddings to see some of the weddings I have photographed, and Info - Photographers to read more about me, and about team photographer ROBIN ISSAC.
Being allowed to be the one to immortalize the big day of a bride and groom is a great honor. And a big responsibility. There is a lot going on this day, and I want to be there and document small and big moments, while being as least visible as possible. My experience provides the security needed on one of the biggest days of their lives. Then you can only focus on enjoying your day to the fullest with family and friends!
I live in chennai, and team photographer robin issac lives in chennai. We take assignments in and around Molde and Bergen, and preferably elsewhere in the country. Costs for travel/accommodation will then be added.
