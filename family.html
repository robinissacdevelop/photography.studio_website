MOMENTS WITH YOUR FAMILY

CHILD PORTRAIT


Taking pictures of children is incredibly fun - and challenging! A wise head once said: "Never work with children or animals"! I have so far stayed away from animals, but children are so much fun to photograph, that I take up the challenge! With two little boys myself, I have had to worry many times, but when it works and you get the good picture of a child, there is hardly anything more rewarding. Excellent when you want great photos for e.g. Christmas cards, calendars, thank you cards etc PRICES FOR CHILD PHOTOGRAPHY: INR. 1300 / 100 INRper additional child The price includes planning, photography for up to 1 hour, as well as post-production with images and an online gallery. Files are bypassed.

FAMILY PHOTO

Investing in a family photo shoot is just that, an investment. Gathering the whole family and having pictures taken and immortalizing the interaction between you is something that is irreplaceable and not something you regret having done. Family photography is a fun and casual time which we also try to fill with a bit of play and laughter, especially for the children :) Perfect when you want great photos for e.g. gifts, Christmas cards, calendars, thank you cards etc. The photos are yours for free private use and to be developed and enlarged as you wish.
