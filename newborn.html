MOMENTS WITH YOUR INFANT

NEWBORN PHOTOGRAPHY

The first weeks at home with a newborn baby are a very special time in life. I am happy to be able to offer newborn photography, which is done in peace and quiet in a safe environment in your own home, a so-called "newborn lifestyle" photography. Here there are less posed pictures, and more focus on the baby together with parents/family at home. We set aside plenty of time for photography, breastfeeding/feeding and cuddling, usually around 1.5-2 hours. This photo shoot should not be stressful, but a good experience to create memories that you can look back on later.
